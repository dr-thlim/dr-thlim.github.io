Introduction

Hi there, I am Lim Tiong Hoo, a Senior Assistant Professor specialising in Artificial Intelligence. I have working in Universiti Teknologi Brunei for almost 20 years and had 6 years industries experiences prior to UTB.

I also hold the Director post in the university Planning and Developing Office under the chancellery, overlooking the university's Strategic Plan and the university's Ranking and Rating.

I was awarded PhD in Computer Science from University of York 2014. I also obtained MSc in Engineering from University of Sheffield and BSc in Computer Science from Keele University. I have a postgraduate teaching certicate from UBD. My achievement includes:

  • 2023 - Research Excellence Mid-Career Academic, Universiti Teknologi Brunei Special Mention, Faculty of Engineering
  • 2023 - Asia Pacific ICT Awards – Aiquatic Merit Award for IoT Project of the year
  • 2023 - Brunei ICT Awards – Aiquatic Merit Award for Research and Development
  • 2022 - Administrative Excellence Award, Universiti Teknologi Brunei For academic staff as Director of Planning and Development Office
  • 2023 - ITEX 2023 Gold Medal Project - DERETINA: Smartphone Fundus Imaging for Intelligent Detection of Eye Diseases (Diabetic Retinopathy),
